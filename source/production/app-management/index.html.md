Production Nanobox apps are managed through the Nanobox dashboard. While much of the functionality necessary for managing a production application is available through the [CLI](/production/cli/) and Boxfile, the dashboard acts as a centralized management plane. It provides everything from service resource statistics and logs to domain and SSL management.
